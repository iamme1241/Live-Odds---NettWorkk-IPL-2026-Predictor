Equalizer Late-joiner multiplier
Formula: M = 70 / (71 − joinMatch)
For wins only, the player earns points × M instead of points. The extra points × (M−1) is added as an equalizer bonus. This statistically brings them to the same expected position as a Match 1 player by Match 70.

Examples: Join M31 → 1.75× · Join M34 → 1.89× · Join M40 → 2.26× · Join M50 → 3.33×
