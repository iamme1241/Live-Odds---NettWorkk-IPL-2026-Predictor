How odds work
Every vote adds 100 points to the pool.
If your team wins, you share the entire pool with other correct predictors.
Fewer votes on your team = higher odds. More votes = lower odds.
Example: Pool of 700 points — 5 pick SRH, 2 pick RCB → SRH wins = 140 points each. RCB wins = 350 points each.
